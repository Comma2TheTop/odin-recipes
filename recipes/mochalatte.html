Mocha Latte Recipe

Description

A mocha latte is a delicious coffee drink that combines the bold flavor of espresso with the creaminess of steamed milk and the sweetness of chocolate. Often topped with whipped cream and sometimes dusted with cocoa powder, it’s a cozy balance of rich coffee and dessert-like indulgence

Ingredients

  • 1 shot of espresso (or ½ cup strong brewed coffee)
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder (or chocolate syrup)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup steamed milk
  • Whipped cream (optional, for topping)
  • Cocoa powder or chocolate shavings (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Brew the espresso or strong coffee.
  2. Mix cocoa powder or chocolate syrup with sugar in your cup. Pour the hot espresso over it and stir until dissolved.
  3. Steam and froth 1 cup of milk until creamy and slightly foamy.
  4. Pour the steamed milk into the chocolate-espresso mixture and stir gently.
  5. Top with whipped cream and sprinkle with cocoa powder or chocolate shavings (optional).
  6. Serve and enjoy!
